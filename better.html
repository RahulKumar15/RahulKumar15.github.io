POEM- BETTER

Posted by Rahul Kumar

climb out of the hole,
forgot about how much time they stole.
take your broken heart,
let all memories depart.
this is your new beginning,
soon you will be winninng.
at the start things will be hard,
believing you're broken and scarred.
holding on to past,
that wasn't meant to last.
sleepless in the night,
thinking you just might,
break into million pieces,
as your depression and anxity releases,
into the dark air,
but out of you window you noticed a golden glare.

catching your eys it's the moonlight,
reminding you to win this fight.
because you are strong,
and loosing you in this world would be so wrong.
as the time passes you won't recall,
those lonely night's when you would bawl.
it will feel like a past life left behind,
one day walking by the sea,
you will feel so free.
as you turn the corner,
you see another mourner.
you will reach out your hand,
sit down together in the sand.
tell them your glory,
you look up to the sky,
a shooting star passes by.
as if the universe sent you a letter,
saying you deserve so much 'BETTER,
