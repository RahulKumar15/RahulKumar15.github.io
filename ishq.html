CHALA THA ZINDGI BHAR EK RAAH MEIN,
BAS TUJJHE PAANE KI CAAH MEIN.
JE ZAZBA ISHQ THA YE JAANTA THA,
TU SIRF MERI THI YE MAANTA THA.
WO IKTARFA THI MOHABAT, WO IKTARFA THA IQRAAR
IKTARFAA THI MERI IBADAT, JISE RAKHA THA BARKARAR.
MERE ASHQ PAANI BAN BAHE THE, PAR HASSIL NAA HUA MUJHE TERA PYAAR.
NAA ISHQ PAR YAKEEN HAI AB, NAA TUJHPE HAI AITBAAR.
NAA IJAAT BACHI NAA ANNA, HAR LAMHA HUA MAKHWAAR.
MERI MAUT KI BOLI LAGI, KARIDAAR MILE HAZAAR.
MAINE KHUD SE JAYADA TUJHE CAAHA THA, TUJHE DEKH KHUDKO SANBHALA THA.
TUJHE PAA LIYA PHIR KHO DIYA, YE SOCH SAITAANAT KO PALA THA.
MERI ISHQ THI MERI SABSE BADI NAADANI, SAMAJ NAA SAKEGI TU MERI KURBANI.
PAR AB MERI AANKHEN KABHI NAA HOGI NAM, TOOTA HU ITTNA BHUL GAYA HU SARRE GUM.
MERI MOHABAAT KA TUNE DUM GHOOTA NA, AAJ SE TUJHE PAANE KA FITOOR KHATAM.

MERI SOCH MERI CAAH MERI ROOH MERI RAAH MERA HAR INSAAN SE SACHA RABTA SAB GALAT.
MERI DIN MERI RAAT MERE TOOTE JAZBAAT MERA SACH MERA WAQT MERE SAQT HALAAT SAB JHOOT.
MEIN BIKHRU TOOTU YE TUJHE MANJOOR, TANHA REHENE KO TUNE KIYA MAJBOOR
TUJHE LAGA TERA RASQ MUJHE KAREGA DUR, TOOTA THA MEIN JAISE WAISE TOOTEGA TERA GUROOR.
MERE BAARE MEIN KAHA THA, TERE BAARE MEIN SUNA THA.
MAINE SAPNE BUNE THE, TUNE APNA CUNNA THA.
HUA WAHI SAB KUCH, JO KABHI NAA HONA THA
KHOYA MAINE HAR LAMHA JO MUJHE NAA KHONA THA.
BANA PHIR MEIN SAITAAN, JAAGA PHIR MERA HAIWAAN
TERE KHILLAF JUNG KAA PHIR HUA AILAAN.
TO AB DAR MUHSE TU MERI BAAT SE, MERE BIKHARTE HUE HAR JAZBAAT SE.v TU DAR MERE MAULA SE MERE JAAT SE.

MERE MAULA MUJHE DE MAAFI, MERI IBAATDAT NHAI HAI KAAFI
MUJHE WO RAAH DIKHA DE, JISSE KAR SAKU MEIN TALAFI
MERI ZINDGI BADI CHOTI HAI MERE GUNAAH AZEEM HAI
TUHI TO MERI RABA DUA, TUHI MERA HQEEM HAI
MERE MAULA EK TUHI TOH SABSE BADA RAHEEM HAI
EK TUJHSE HI TO MANGUGA TU HI TOH BADA KAREEM HAI
MAINE ISHQ MADIN CHODA THA MUJHE PHIR TU APNE DHANG DE
TERE SAAMNE HAATH PHAILAYA HU MUJHE APNE RANG MEIN RANG DE
MUJHE HIMMAT DE MUJHE TAAQAT DE, MUJHE DIN IMMANE IBAADAT DE
TERI NAAM KI TASWIR ROZ PADHU MERE ROOH KO TU YE AADAT DE
TERE NAAM SE SURAJ KO DEKHU TERE NAAM KE SANG MERI SAAM DHALE
MERA HUMSAFAR BAS TUHI HU TERA KALMA MERE SAAATH CHALE
MUJHE KAAFI MUSHRIKH BOLA THA MERE AGH KO HARAAM KARAAR DIYA
JAB CHAAR NE MERI BURAAI KI TO USNE NAA INQAAR KIYA
YA MAULA TUNE SAB DEKHA AB USKO BHI DIKHA DE
JISNE MUJHKO TOODA THA TU USKO TOOTNA SIKHA DE
MERE PYAAR KO JHOOTHA BOLA THA
MUJHE DHOKE KI NAJAR SE TOOLA THA
MAINE RAAZ JO USKO BATTAE THE, USNE SABKE SAAME KHOLA THA
AB MAULA MUJHE BAS TERA HONA HAI
MERI ROOH KO TU AISA DHAAL DE
AUR TUJHSE BHIK HU MAANTA
USKI ZINDGI SE ISHQ KO NIKAAL DE
